# Rethicssec – Purpose & Vision

Rethicssec is a cybersecurity companion designed for Africa. Its purpose is to make digital safety simple, accessible, and trustworthy for everyone — from first‑time internet users to community organizations — by combining safe incident reporting, practical guidance, and education in one multilingual app.

## Why It Exists

Digital adoption across Africa is accelerating, but access to trusted, local, and easy‑to‑use safety tools has not kept pace. People are exposed to scams, fraud, phishing, harassment, identity theft, and other online harms without clear, actionable support. Rethicssec closes this gap by offering:

- A safe, confidential way to report cyber incidents and receive help
- An AI assistant that explains risks and next steps in plain language
- A threat scanner to quickly check suspicious links, messages, or files
- Practical education to build day‑to‑day digital resilience
- Emergency contacts and pathways to escalate when time matters

## Who It Serves

- Individuals and families seeking simple guidance and protection
- Community organizations that support vulnerable users
- Schools and youth programs building digital literacy
- Civil society partners and responders coordinating assistance

## Problems We Solve

- “I think I’ve been scammed — what do I do now?”
- “Is this message/link real or fake?”
- “How can I report an incident safely and track progress?”
- “Where can I learn to stay safe without technical jargon?”
- “Who can help me right now in an emergency?”

## What Success Looks Like

- Lower victimization through early detection and better decisions
- Faster, safer reporting with clear, empathetic guidance
- Growing digital literacy and community resilience
- Responsible data handling that respects privacy and consent

## Guiding Principles

- Privacy‑by‑Design: minimal data, transparent choices, secure storage
- Safety First: clear content warnings, trusted flows, crisis paths
- Accessibility & Inclusion: works for non‑technical users and low‑resource devices
- Cultural Relevance: local language support and regional context
- Transparency & Control: users own their choices and data

## What’s in the App

- Incident Reporting: structured, confidential reports with progress tracking
- AI Assistant: plain‑language answers and safety guidance
- Threat Scanner: quick checks for URLs, emails, and files
- Education Hub: practical tips for everyday safety
- Emergency Contacts: fast access to local support and resources

## Languages (Multilingual by Default)

English, Swahili, French, Arabic, Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo, Zulu, Xhosa, Afrikaans, and Sawa (Duala).

Note: Sawa (Duala) is included and curated manually; most machine translation engines do not fully support it.

## Typical Journey

1) Choose your language and sign in
2) See a clear dashboard of tools and recent activity
3) Report an incident or ask the AI assistant what to do
4) Scan suspicious content to get a fast safety verdict
5) Learn simple, high‑impact tips to stay protected
6) Reach emergency contacts when immediate help is needed

## Respect for Users

Rethicssec treats every user with dignity and respect. We aim to reduce harm, not amplify it. The product avoids dark patterns, avoids over‑collection of data, and provides clear explanations in familiar terms.

If you have suggestions to improve clarity, cultural relevance, or language quality — especially for African languages — your contributions are welcome.
