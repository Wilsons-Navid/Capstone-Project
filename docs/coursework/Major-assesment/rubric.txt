Assignment Description: 

In this assignment, you will compile a comprehensive research proposal on your topic. The proposal should be structured into three chapters, each addressing a specific aspect of the research. The proposal should demonstrate critical thinking, coherence, and a deep understanding of the subject matter (literature review, research objectives, and outline of their research methodology ). 

Assignment purpose: The primary purpose of this assignment is to equip you with the necessary skills to formulate a well-structured research proposal.

Formatting tips
Font style: Times New Roman for the body text. Arial, or Helvetica for headings and subheadings. 
Font size: 12-point font for the body of text. Headings and subheadings may be slightly larger (14 points for main headings, 12 points for subheadings.
Line spacing: Double spacing is preferred for the body text. Single-spacing can be used for block quotes, captions, and references
Alignment: Body text is aligned 'justified'.
Margins: Standard margins of 1 inch on all sides.
